Nepal is a captivating destination with breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage. Nestled in the Himalayas, it offers stunning views of snow-capped peaks like Mount Everest. Trekking trails such as the Annapurna Circuit and Everest Base Camp immerse travelers in nature and local culture. Beyond the mountains, Nepal boasts lush jungles and serene lakes like Chitwan National Park and Phewa Lake. The vibrant culture, ancient temples, and festivals of the Kathmandu Valley make Nepal an unforgettable destination for adventure and tranquility.
